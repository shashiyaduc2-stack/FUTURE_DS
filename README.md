#  Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to extract meaningful insights and support data-driven decision-making. The dashboard provides a clear view of revenue trends, product performance, and regional sales distribution.

The objective is to simulate a real-world business scenario where a data analyst helps stakeholders understand performance and identify growth opportunities.

---

##  Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Understand regional sales distribution
- Track sales trends over time
- Provide actionable business insights

---

##  Tools Used
- Power BI (Data visualization & dashboard creation)
- Microsoft Excel / CSV (Data source)

---

##  Key KPIs
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Total Quantity Sold  
- Total Discount  

---

##  Dashboard Features
- **Sales by Region** – Identifies high-performing regions  
- **Profit by State** – Highlights profitable locations  
- **Sales by Customer** – Shows top customers  
- **Quantity by Region** – Displays demand distribution  
- **Discount Analysis** – Understands impact of discounts  

---

##  Key Insights
- The **Technology category** generates the highest revenue  
- The **West region** shows strong overall performance  
- Sales show a **steady growth trend over time**  
- Some products have **high sales but low profit margins**  

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Focus on **high-profit categories** to increase overall profitability  
- Optimize pricing strategies for **low-margin products**  
- Expand marketing efforts in **top-performing regions**  
- Reduce excessive discounts to improve profit  

---

##  Dataset
The dataset used represents a typical retail business scenario including:
- Orders
- Sales
- Profit
- Customers
- Regions
